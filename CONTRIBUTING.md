## Ways You Can Contribute

While DocuWrite Pro is not an open-governance project, constructive involvement is welcome in the following forms:

### 1. Feature Requests
You may suggest:
- New tools or enhancements
- UI/UX improvements
- Performance or accessibility ideas

Suggestions should be **clear, respectful, and practical**.  
There is no guarantee that all requests will be implemented.

---

### 2. Bug Reports
If you discover a bug:
- Describe the issue clearly
- Include steps to reproduce (if possible)
- Mention the browser/device/environment used

Incomplete or vague reports may not be addressed.

---

### 3. Feedback & Improvements
Thoughtful feedback is appreciated, especially when it focuses on:
- User experience
- Workflow efficiency
- Educational or productivity value

Feedback should aim to **improve**, not to criticize destructively.
