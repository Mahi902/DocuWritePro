The perks of having a
DocuWrite Pro account
One tap with Google unlocks a smoother, safer, and more connected way to work with your documents.
password
No emails, no passwords
Skip the sign-up form entirely. Just continue with the Google account you already use — nothing new to create, nothing new to forget.
verified_user
No third-party servers, ever
We never ask you to trust a storage server you've never heard of. Your documents are saved straight to your own Google Drive — a place you already trust.
cloud_done
Your documents, everywhere
Start writing on your laptop, pick up right where you left off on your phone. Everything lives safely in the cloud and follows you, not the other way around.
folder_shared
Effortless sharing, both ways
Every document you create appears directly in your Google Drive, ready to share in seconds. And anything shared with you elsewhere in Drive shows up right inside DocuWrite Pro automatically — no importing, no exporting.
workspace_premium
Exclusive roles & identity
Signed-in members can unlock special roles and a distinct identity badge inside the app — a small mark of being part of the DocuWrite Pro community.
No hidden costs. No new accounts. Just your Google account, working harder for you.
