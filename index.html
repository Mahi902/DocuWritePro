Assistive Writing.
Your Silent Co-Author.

Every feature in DocuWrite Pro's Assistive Writing panel runs entirely in your browser — no API calls, no data leaving your device, no subscriptions. Just smarter writing, privately.

Auto Assist

Context-aware writing suggestions that activate as you type — think of it as autocomplete with editorial intelligence.

Spell Check

Underlines misspelled words in real time across your entire document as you write.

Auto Spell Corrector

Automatically fixes common spelling errors on the fly — no manual review needed.

Grammar Checker

Powered by Harper WASM — inline grammar highlights that run offline and privately, with zero server contact.

Repair Grammar

One-click fix-all button that corrects all detected grammar issues across the full document instantly.

Word Prediction

Suggests the next word as you write, learning from context to keep your flow uninterrupted.

Full Document Translation

Translate your entire document into another language in one action — no copy-paste, no switching tabs.

Auto Placeholders

Smart placeholder text fills in structural gaps while you draft, keeping your document shape intact.

Offensive Content Detector

Flags potentially harmful or inappropriate language in your document before you share it — keeping your writing professional and inclusive.

4.5 App Rating

DocuWrite Pro installs as a Progressive Web App on any device — Android, iPhone, or desktop. No app store. No download. Just open and install.

Android — Chrome
  1. Open Chrome and visit mahi902.github.io/DocuWritePro
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right corner)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" and confirm
  4. The app icon appears on your home screen — done.
iPhone / iPad — Safari
  1. Open Safari and visit the site (must be Safari)
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow, bottom bar)
  3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — app is now on your home screen.
Desktop — Chrome or Edge
  1. Open Chrome or Edge and visit the site
  2. Look for the install icon ⊕ in the address bar
  3. Click it and select "Install"
  4. DocuWrite Pro opens as a standalone window.
