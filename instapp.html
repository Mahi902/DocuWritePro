How to Install (PWA)

desktop_windows On Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Look for the "Install" icon in the address bar (right side).
  2. Click it and then click "Install" in the prompt.
  3. The app will be added to your desktop or start menu.

phone_android On Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. Confirm the installation.

phone_iphone On iOS (Safari)

  1. Tap the "Share" icon (a square with an arrow) in the bottom bar.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Confirm the name and tap "Add".
