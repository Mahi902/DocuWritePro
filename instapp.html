How to Install DocuWrite Pro

DocuWrite Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it directly from your browser without an app store.

On Chrome / Android:

  1. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner.
  2. Select "Install app".
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts.

On Safari / iOS:

  1. Tap the Share icon () at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
